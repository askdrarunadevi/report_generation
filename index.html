Personalized Preventive Education Protocols

Standard General Oral Hygiene Directives

  • Execute the Modified Bass Brushing Technique at a 45° tooth axis.
  • Brush twice daily for a minimum duration of 2 full minutes.
  • Clean the dorsal tongue surface once daily with a specialized tool.
  • Floss interdental gaps once daily to disrupt microbial plaque.
  • Rinse thoroughly with clean water immediately after every single food session.
  • Maintain continuous hydration profiles with adequate daily water intake.
  • Reduce regular consumption frequencies of highly refined sugars and sodas.
  • Avoid smoking, vaping, gutka, pan, or any raw tobacco products completely.
  • Replace physical toothbrushes completely every 3 months.
  • Secure regular clinical scalings every 6 months cleanly.
